About Companies Act 2013

The Companies Act 2013 governs the incorporation, responsibilities, and functioning of companies in India. It aims to improve corporate governance, simplify regulations, and protect stakeholders. The Act has introduced key reforms, including provisions for corporate social responsibility and enhanced accountability measures.
